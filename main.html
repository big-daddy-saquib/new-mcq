Who We Are

Dr Puto is a medical student education platform helping students prepare for university exams, USMLE, FMGE, and medical licensing exams in Dubai, Qatar, and Saudi Arabia.

We provide quality Qbanks, past year questions (PYQs), notes, and clinical improvement guidance. Plus, we offer full counseling for your medical journey and host research communities, meet-ups, Sunday fun functions, and educational conferences.
