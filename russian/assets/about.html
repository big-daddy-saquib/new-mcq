Who We Are

Dr. Pluto is a global medical teaching platform dedicated to guiding students toward success in US and international medical universities. We combine academic excellence with practical exposure to build confident, competent future doctors.

Our programs are designed for medical aspirants preparing for university admissions, licensing exams, and professional growth across multiple countries.

What We Do

University Guidance

Support for US and international medical admissions.

Exam Preparation

USMLE, FMGE, and global medical licensing exams.

Workshops & Talks

Interactive sessions led by experienced medical professionals.

Research Exposure

Hands on academic research guidance with expert mentors.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower medical students with the knowledge, skills, and confidence required to succeed globally while maintaining academic integrity and professional ethics.

Our Community

Dr. Pluto is more than a teaching platform. We foster a collaborative student community through discussions, peer learning, fun clubs, and academic engagement activities that promote continuous improvement.
